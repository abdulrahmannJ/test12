Permit Types Available

Choose the permit that matches your project. Apply and pay online within minutes with our streamlined process.

💼

Occupational Permits

Essential permits for businesses and professionals operating within MSA County boundaries.

Requirements Include:

  • Proof of professional qualifications
  • Business registration documents
  • Insurance and compliance certificates
🎯 Apply for Occupational Permit
🔨

Demolition Permits

Required for full or partial structure removal projects with safety protocols.

Requirements Include:

  • Detailed site and demolition plans
  • Safety measures documentation
  • Waste management and disposal plan
🏗️ Apply for Demolition Permit
🧱

Hoarding Permits

For temporary structures and safety hoarding around construction sites.

Requirements Include:

  • Hoarding design and specifications
  • Project timeline and duration
  • Public safety and access plan
🚧 Apply for Hoarding Permit
🗑️

Dumping Permits

Authorized material dumping permits for designated county disposal areas.

Requirements Include:

  • Material type and quantity details
  • Approved disposal location info
  • Environmental compliance documentation
♻️ Apply for Dumping Permit
